About This Application

This application was designed to help managers and employees efficiently handle work-related tasks such as tracking timesheets, managing leave requests, and communicating effectively through a built-in messaging system.

It streamlines day-to-day operations by providing a clear interface to submit, review, and approve employee data. The goal is to save time, improve transparency, and ensure accurate record keeping across the organization.

Whether you're submitting your hours, checking your team's availability, or reviewing internal feedback, this platform is built to support your workflow with clarity and ease.
